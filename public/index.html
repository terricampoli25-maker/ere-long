How to Use

Ere Long keeps four faces — four separate events. Turn between them with the ◀ ▶ arrows or your keyboard's arrow keys. Name an event, choose its date and hour, and press Set.

Await marks something longed for; Forebode, something dreaded. The scenery, music, and words follow the mood as the hour draws near. Choose neither, and all stays still. The ♪ button opens the music settings — silence everything, choose how long arrival music plays, or listen to the two themes.

Your Events

Private by design — your serial number only unlocks the door. Your events are saved solely in this browser, on this device: no account, no database of your countdowns, and no one — not even us — can see what you're counting down to. But if you clear your browser data or switch devices, your events will be lost. So:

Export Events — saves all four of your countdowns to a file on your computer. Keep it somewhere safe.

Import Events — restores your countdowns from a previously exported file. Use this on a new device or after clearing your data.
